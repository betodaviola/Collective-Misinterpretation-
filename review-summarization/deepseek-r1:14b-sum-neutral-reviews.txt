The piece for viola and fixed media is an exploration of tension and contrast, blending organic and electronic elements in a way that challenges traditional boundaries. The live viola performance, with its rich, expressive tones, often soars above the processed electronics, creating a dynamic interplay between the human and the technological. The harmonic shifts are abrupt and unexpected, adding to the piece's disorienting yet intriguing quality.

The use of silence is striking, amplifying the tension and allowing the sounds to breathe. While technically impressive, some reviews suggest the piece may feel overly abstract or emotionally distant for those seeking immediate resonance.
