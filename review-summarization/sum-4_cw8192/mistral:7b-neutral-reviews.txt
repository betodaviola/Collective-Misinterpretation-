The structure of the piece was cyclical, with recurring motifs that shifted and transformed, giving a sense of familiarity amidst innovation. Each element—the viola, fixed media, and the dynamic interplay between them—wove together seamlessly to create an immersive soundscape that was as thought-provoking as it was beautiful.
