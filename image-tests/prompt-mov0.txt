The piece, performed on viola and fixed media, unfolds as a captivating exploration of organic and electronic sounds. A haunting melody from the viola weaves through layers of shimmering electronics, creating tension and release. The interplay between these elements feels like a dynamic dialogue, with the live instrument and pre-recorded material responding to each other intuitively. The harmonies are dissonant yet evocative, shifting unexpectedly while maintaining an overall reflective mood. Rhythmic patterns emerge subtly, defying traditional meter and adding unpredictability to the music. The structure of the piece is fluid, with moments of silence punctuating the soundscape to great effect. Overall, it is a challenging but intriguing listen, pushing boundaries while leaving listeners questioning the interplay between tradition and modernity.
