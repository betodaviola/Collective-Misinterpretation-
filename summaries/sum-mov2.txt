 This piece for viola and fixed media is a captivating blend of organic and electronic sounds that creates a hauntingly beautiful yet introspective atmosphere. The viola's melodies weave intricately through a rich tapestry of electronic textures, with the fixed media responding intuitively to the live instrument's phrasing. The harmony is both complex and evocative, with dissonant intervals occasionally resolving into unexpected beauty.

The structure is fluid, with sudden shifts in energy and mood that keep the listener engaged. The interplay between the two mediums feels deliberate yet spontaneous, as if they are engaging in a conversation that explores tension and release. Each sound choice carefully navigates the listener through shifting emotions, from moments of serenity to bursts of vibrant energy.

By the end, the piece leaves an impression of quiet innovation and emotional resonance, blending technical precision with introspective depth. The immersive soundscape invites both reflection and engagement, offering a unique listening experience that feels both familiar and entirely new.
