 This contemporary piece for viola and fixed media is a haunting and captivating exploration of emotional landscapes, with electronic sounds subtly woven around the organic tones of the viola. The interplay between these two elements creates an intimate yet expansive atmosphere, as the performer skillfully manipulates their instrument to produce ethereal harmonics and delicate glissandos.

The piece unfolds as a dynamic dialogue between tension and release, with shifting dynamics and harmony that keeps the listener on edge. The fixed media responds to the viola's melodic phrases with intricate layers of processed sounds, creating a sense of depth and spatiality. Despite its experimental nature, there is undeniable emotional depth, evoking feelings of mystery, wonder, and introspection.

The overall effect is one of captivating beauty, showcasing both technical mastery and raw expressivity. The blend of traditional instrumentation with modern technology creates a soundscape that is simultaneously familiar and otherworldly, leaving a lasting impression on the audience.
