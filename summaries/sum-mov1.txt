 This piece for viola and fixed media is a mesmerizing exploration of sound that masterfully blends organic and electronic elements. The haunting melodies of the viola weave through layers of electronic textures, creating an immersive soundscape that feels both intimate and expansive. Fixed media components respond intuitively to the viola's phrasing, adding depth and complexity with subtle drones, processed sounds, and rhythmic pulses.

The overall atmosphere is contemplative yet vibrant, with unexpected shifts in dynamics and harmony keeping the listener engaged. The piece strikes a delicate balance between technical innovation and emotional resonance, balancing moments of stillness and bursts of energy. It's an intricate conversation between organic and electronic sounds, blurring the line between performer and technology to create something uniquely beautiful.

The music evokes a sense of introspection and wonder, inviting reflection on its complex soundscape without sacrificing accessibility. While it may challenge traditional expectations, the piece leaves a lasting impression with its thought-provoking blend of live instrumentation and fixed media, offering a captivating auditory experience that transcends genre boundaries.
