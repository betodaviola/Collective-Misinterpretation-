 This piece for viola and fixed media is a mesmerizing exploration of contrasting sounds, seamlessly blending organic melodies with electronic textures. The viola's melody, rich in emotion, weaves through the piece, echoed and expanded by the processed electronics. These manipulated sounds create an immersive atmosphere that shifts between intimacy and expansiveness.

The fixed media elements respond intuitively to the viola, introducing moments of tension and release as they mirror or disrupt its phrasing. The harmony is unpredictable yet captivating, shifting between consonant and dissonant intervals, leaving a lingering sense of unease or curiosity. Silences serve as punctuation points, allowing the listener to absorb the emotional weight of each section.

Throughout the piece, the viola's warm tones merge with the electronic textures, creating hybrid timbres that feel both familiar and alien. The overall mood oscillates between introspection and hope, inviting the listener on an emotional journey through shifting soundscapes. By the end, the composition leaves a lasting impression of its unique character and innovative approach to contemporary music.
